How the sector works

Four parties make every supported-housing deal.

The directory maps the part you care about — who operates where, and how to reach them.

01 · COMMISSIONERS

Councils & NHS bodies

Fund the care and refer service users to approved providers. Demand starts here.

02 · PROVIDERS

Supported-living operators

Approved to deliver care, and tasked with sourcing the housing for every referral.

03 · THE LEASE

Long, guaranteed rent

A registered provider leases the property, sets the rent and handles housing benefit.

04 · THE LANDLORD

Owners & developers

Supply the homes the sector is short of — on long, fixed-term leases to a company, not an individual.

FAQ

Straight answers.

What is the directory?

A searchable, England-wide directory of the supported-living and social-housing providers commissioned in each area — with the commissioners behind them, the care they deliver, and verified contact details. Search by postcode, borough or county.

What do I get for £29.99?

Full access to every provider listing in your chosen area — names, commissioners, care type and verified contacts — plus outreach templates and a usage guide.

How current is the data?

The directory is refreshed monthly and contacts are verified against live websites and Companies House before listing.

Do you broker deals between me and a provider?

No — we’re a directory and research tool, not a broker. We show you who’s active and how to reach them; any agreement is between you and the provider.
